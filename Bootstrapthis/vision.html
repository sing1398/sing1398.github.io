Our Vision

Our vision is to win souls through the preaching, teaching and witnessing to them the word of god and baptizing them in the name of Jesus Christ. To make true disciples of our members by educating and training our members so that they may go and spread his word wherever they go. The church strives to win souls. “where there is no vision, the people perish: but he that keepeth the law, happy is he.” proverbs 29:18

We believe the Holy Scriptures to be the divinely inbreathed, infallible, inerrant and authoritative Word of God.

We believe in Christ’s Lordship of the Church, the observance of the ordinances of Christian baptism by immersion for believers, and the Lord’s Supper.

We believe in the eternal blessedness of the redeemed in heaven and the eternal doom of the unregenerate in the lake of fire.
